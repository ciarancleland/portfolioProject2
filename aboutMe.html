My History

I had been living in America for the last 8 years. Before that though I grew up in Castlewellan, Co Down, Ireland. Look it's right here!

Growing up I played different sports, but my main focus was on Karate, where I achieved numerous trophies and medals, and was fortunate enough to earn my 1st Dan Black Belt at the age of 17.

In my early years I was a student of St Malachys Primary School, before moving to St Louis Grammar School for my secondary levelk educations. A big thanks to those teachers who helped me throughout.

I studied at St Marys University Belfast to become a Secondary School Teacher, but that idea was short lived! The teaching world was just not for me. It was shortly after this at the age of 21 I moved to America

While living in America I had the privledge of moving to different states throughout my time. Florida, North Carolina and Pennsylvania. Each showing me some interesting places and people.

I found myself in the world of Country Clubs working within the Hospitality Sector, and worked my way up to a Directorship level by the time leaving the industry. The 1 area I truely fell in love with throughout this was Bars; their creation, development and management. Along with the Wine. Waow I had my eyes opened!
